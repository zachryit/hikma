GHANA EXCELS AT AFRICAN ABACUS OLYMPIAD IN RWANDA

March 10, 2025 AHIMS Admin

Team Ghana, represented by Shenmo Ghana, swept the top awards at the prestigious second African Shenmo Cup, International Abacus Mental Olympiad held in Kigali, Rwanda.

Add featured image here

Historic Achievement for Ghana

In a remarkable display of mental arithmetic prowess, Ghanaian students dominated the second African Shenmo Cup, International Abacus Mental Olympiad. The competition, hosted in Kigali, Rwanda, brought together the brightest young minds from across the African continent.

Al-Hikmah Islamic Montessori School proudly represented Ghana through our partnership with Shenmo Education Ghana. Our students demonstrated exceptional skills in mental calculation, problem-solving, and concentration that left judges and spectators in awe.

Add competition image here

The Power of Abacus Training

The abacus is not just an ancient calculating tool; it's a powerful educational instrument that enhances cognitive development. Through systematic training, our students develop:

  • Enhanced concentration and focus
  • Improved memory and retention capabilities
  • Faster mental calculation abilities
  • Better problem-solving skills
  • Increased confidence in mathematics

Add training image here

Outstanding Performances

Our students competed in various age categories, showcasing their abilities in both physical abacus manipulation and mental arithmetic. The competition included speed calculations, accuracy tests, and complex problem-solving challenges.

"Witnessing our students compete at this international level fills us with immense pride. Their dedication, discipline, and hard work have truly paid off."

Head of Academics, AHIMS

Add awards ceremony image here

Shenmo Partnership

Our partnership with Shenmo Education has brought world-class abacus training to AHIMS. The Shenmo method combines traditional abacus techniques with modern educational psychology, creating a comprehensive program that develops both mathematical skills and overall cognitive abilities.

This achievement at the African Olympiad demonstrates the effectiveness of our academic programs and our commitment to providing students with diverse learning opportunities that prepare them for global competition.

Add team photo here

Looking Forward

This success motivates us to continue investing in innovative educational programs. We congratulate all our students who participated and thank their parents, teachers, and the Shenmo Ghana team for their unwavering support.

As we celebrate this achievement, we remain committed to nurturing academic excellence and preparing our students to excel on both national and international platforms.
