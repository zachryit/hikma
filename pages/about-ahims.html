About Al-Hikmah Islamic Montessori School

Our Institution

Welcome to Al-Hikmah Islamic Montessori School

To nurture confident, creative, and compassionate learners by integrating Montessori pedagogy with Islamic values (adab, ihsan, amanah), enabling every child to grow spiritually, intellectually, socially, and emotionally—prepared to serve society with excellence.

AHIMS delivers a unique hybrid curriculum combining the Montessori framework with core elements of the Ghana Education Service curriculum—enriched by Islamic Studies (Tawhid, Fiqh, Seerah) and Arabic/Qur'anic literacy. Our prepared environments are designed for independence, discovery, and differentiated instruction.

Our Educational Programs

Early Years

Nursery & Kindergarten

Our early years program harnesses learner independence and appreciation of personal responsibility through:

  • Montessori Practical Life & Sensorial activities
  • Islamic character education (adab, amanah)
  • Arabic phonics and Qur'anic recitation
  • Child-centered discovery and mastery-based learning
  • Prepared environments for independence
"Never help a child with a task at which he feels he can succeed." - Maria Montessori

Learning Journey

Lower Primary Education

We offer a complete educational pathway designed to develop well-rounded, values-based learners:

Montessori Language & Mathematics

Hands-on, self-paced learning with manipulatives and mastery-based progression
