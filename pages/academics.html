Our Curriculum Approach

Islamic-Montessori Hybrid Curriculum

At Al-Hikmah Islamic Montessori School, we deliver a unique hybrid curriculum combining Montessori pedagogy with Islamic values and the Ghana Education Service curriculum. Our prepared environments enable self-paced, hands-on learning while nurturing spiritual, intellectual, and character development.

Montessori hands-on materials
Islamic character formation (adab, ihsan, amanah)
Child-led discovery and mastery-based learning
Arabic & Qur'anic literacy

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in holistic education that develops students' talents and interests beyond the classroom

Robotics Club

Our Robotics club has a budding modern ICT laboratory with laptops, projectors, sound systems, robotics tools, and other essentials for effective learning.

Robotics club is subdivided into coding and hardware classes to provide students with skills to develop and program their own robots.

Sports & Athletics

Our sports program includes football, basketball, athletics, and other activities promoting teamwork, discipline, and healthy living.

We participate in inter-school competitions with facilities to nurture athletic talents alongside academics.
