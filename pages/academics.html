Extracurricular Activities

We believe in holistic education that develops students' talents and interests beyond the classroom

Robotics Club

Our Robotics club has a budding modern ICT laboratory with laptops, projectors, sound systems, robotics tools, and other essentials for effective learning.

Robotics club is subdivided into coding and hardware SSAses to provide students with skills to develop and program their own robots.

Arts Club

Our Art club ignites creativity in learners, giving them confidence to display their talents. We provide a platform for free expression through art and crafts.

Art opens hearts and minds to possibilities, fueling imagination. We create new things and experience art in different ways.

Read2Achieve

We believe reading unlocks children's mastery of any subject. Our #Read2Achieve program instills a strong reading culture among learners and staff.

Each learner is assigned a monthly storybook, supervised to read effectively and produce a review before selecting a new book.

Sports & Athletics

Our sports program includes football, basketball, athletics, and other activities promoting teamwork, discipline, and healthy living.

We participate in inter-school competitions with facilities to nurture athletic talents alongside academics.
