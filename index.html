Welcome Message from the Founder

"The task ahead is great indeed and heavy is the responsibility; and yet it is a noble and glorious challenge – a challenge which calls for the chance to dream, the courage to believe, the courage to draw, the courage to do, the courage to envision, the courage to work, the courage to achieve – to achieve the highest in excellencies. Dare we ask for more in life?"
- Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

As parents and caregivers, we are confronted with many responsibilities. But, towering above them all is the desire to shape our children's future in the most positive and self-sustaining way, by providing them with quality education.

Education is a trusted conduit to move wealth from one generation to the next. Education can also be the great equalizer which uplifts generations of poverty to new heights of distinction and success.

At Al-Hikmah Islamic Montessori School, the prospect of quality education is not lost on us. In fact, it remains our conviction to create a conducive learning environment which will spearhead deep, critical thought in all our students and encourage them to exert maximum effort in all their present and future endeavours.

Paul Osei-Amru

Founder and President
Al-Hikmah Islamic Montessori School

Our Vision

Islamic-Montessori Hybrid

AHIMS uniquely integrates child-led Montessori learning with Qur'anic ethics and Prophetic character, creating confident and compassionate learners.

Holistic Development

We nurture every child's spiritual, intellectual, social-emotional, and physical growth through prepared environments and individualized learning plans.

Character & Values

We anchor education in Islamic values—adab, ihsan, and amanah— fostering students who serve society with integrity and excellence.

National & Global Standards

We blend Montessori pedagogy with Ghana Education Service curriculum standards, preparing students for national progression and global citizenship.

Our Mission

To nurture confident, creative, and compassionate learners by integrating Montessori pedagogy with Islamic values (adab, ihsan, amanah), enabling every child to grow spiritually, intellectually, socially, and emotionally—prepared to serve society with excellence.
