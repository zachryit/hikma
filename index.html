Welcome Message from the Director

"The best among you are those who learn the Qur'an and teach it. Seek knowledge from the cradle to the grave. Indeed, the seeking of knowledge is obligatory upon every Muslim."
- Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him)

As parents and educators, we are entrusted with one of the most sacred responsibilities in Islam – nurturing the hearts and minds of our children. Quality education is not merely academic achievement, but the holistic development of body, mind, and soul.

At Al-Hikmah Islamic Montessori School, we embrace this divine trust (amanah) by blending the child-centered Montessori approach with timeless Islamic values. We believe every child is born with innate potential (fitrah) that deserves to flourish in an environment of love, respect, and purposeful guidance.

Our commitment is to create a learning sanctuary where children develop strong Islamic character, critical thinking, and a love for lifelong learning – preparing them not only for worldly success but also to become compassionate servants of Allah and beneficial members of our ummah.

Maimuna Dkeny Alhassan

Director
Al-Hikmah Islamic Montessori School

Our Vision

Islamic-Montessori Hybrid

AHIMS uniquely integrates child-led Montessori learning with Qur'anic ethics and Prophetic character, creating confident and compassionate learners.

Holistic Development

We nurture every child's spiritual, intellectual, social-emotional, and physical growth through prepared environments and individualized learning plans.

Character & Values

We anchor education in Islamic values—adab, ihsan, and amanah— fostering students who serve society with integrity and excellence.

National & Global Standards

We blend Montessori pedagogy with Ghana Education Service curriculum standards, preparing students for national progression and global citizenship.

Our Mission

To nurture confident, creative, and compassionate learners by integrating Montessori pedagogy with Islamic values (adab, ihsan, amanah), enabling every child to grow spiritually, intellectually, socially, and emotionally—prepared to serve society with excellence.
